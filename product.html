Our Product

Jie yuan's new generation of household energy storage system has two output voltage specifications of 220V and 110V, which can meet the diverse needs of users around the world. The clean energy storage system adopts a modular design, including power modules and battery expansion modules, so it can be easily combined into a system of any capacity according to user needs. The energy storage module adopts lithium iron phosphate battery with excellent performance and long life. At the same time, it adopts modular structure design. Each energy storage module is integrated with the intelligent BMS system and can be easily expanded to form up to 20 Kwh battery packs.
